Anaphylaxis

Allergic reaction, causing swelling of the neck

SYMPTOMS

Victim will have trouble breathing, as their enflamed neck blocks the airway. Hives, rashes, or nausea may occur also.

TREATMENT

Call EMS, as situations can escelate very quickly. Act fast, and find the victim's epi-pen. It is likely in the bag they came with, or with a friend or family member. After administering the epi-pen, aid the arrival of EMS, as the epi-pen only lasts 20 minutes.
