Facial Injuries

Damage to the face, hurts a lot

SYMPTOMS

Victim's injury will likely be very obvious. Attempt to comfort the victim, as they will be in immense pain.

TREATMENT

Clear airways, that could be covered by injury. If a spinal could have been possible, assume it did happen. Put victim into semi-prone, ensuring to watch the airways. Ir airway becomes blocked, use mouth-to-nose for breathing.
