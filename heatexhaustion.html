Heat Exhaustion

Far too much time in the sun

SYMPTOMS

Victim will feel tired, very hot, and will be breathing very fast. Likely victim will be partially conscious, showing much confusion.

TREATMENT

Cool down victim, ensuring to stay in the shade. Drink cool water, and eat chilled foods. If desperate, put ice pack in hot parts of the body, like arm/leg pits.
