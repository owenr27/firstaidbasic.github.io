Closed Chest Wound

Damaged ribs, or an abdominal injury

SYMPTOMS

Victim will have trouble breathing. Cause will often be clear, as a major accident would be required to cause that much harm.

TREATMENT

Ensure the victim is comfortable. Check vitals, and watch for possible unconsciousness. Do not let the victim have food or drink. Encourage the victim to lay/lean on a table or surface, to take strain off of the body.
