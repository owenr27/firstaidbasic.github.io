Burn (Third Degree)

Extremely Severe Burn

SYMPTOMS

Affected area will look burnt, almost charred to a crisp. Can affect nerves, muscles, and bones in the victim. Call 911 immediately, victim will be in severe pain.

TREATMENT

Wash the affected area with room temperature water, progressively going colder and colder. Wrap with gauze, ensuring to separate fingers/toes with small pieces.
