Broken Bone

Fractured Bone

SYMPTOMS

Can be a closed fracture, where the bone it still inside skin. An open fracture is when the bone is poking out of the skin, and will result in much more blood loss.

TREATMENT

Put splints parallel to the broken bone, then wrap tightly with gauze. Let the victim rest their limb, don't let them move it. Hold a cool ice-pack to the injury. Slightly raise the bone, ensuring it is not being moved by the victim.
