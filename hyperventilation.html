Hyperventilation

Often panic-induced quick breathing

SYMPTOMS

Victim will be out of breath, and breathing rapidly. Victim's face will look flushed, and will clearly be panicked.

TREATMENT

Pursed lip breathing - in through the nose and out through pursed lips, “As if blowing down a straw”. Inhale for 4 seconds, exhale 4 seconds. Ask the victim to look at you, in order to calm you down. Treat for shock.
