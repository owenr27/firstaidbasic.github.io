SAMPLE QUESTIONS

Primary assessment, complete first

S - Signs and symptoms: Description of vital signs, visual signs. A - Allergies: Does the victim suffer from any chronic conditions? M - Medication or medical history: Has the victim been to the hospital recently, any medication? P - Past: Medical past, immediate past, and past symptoms. L - Last meal: Empty stomach/possible allergy. E - Events prior: ask “What happened to you?”.
