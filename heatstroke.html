Heat Stroke

Extreme heat exhaustion

SYMPTOMS

Victim will likely be fading in and out of consciousness. Victim will be extremely warm, with a high pulse, and lots of sweating.

TREATMENT

Carefully monitor victim, as seizures and vomiting may occur in extreme cases. Drink cool water, and eat chilled foods. If desperate, put ice pack in hot parts of the body, like arm/leg pits. Monitor vital signs, as consciousness could change any moment.
