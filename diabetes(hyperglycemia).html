Diabetes (Hyperglycemia)

High blood sugar

SYMPTOMS

Victim will have trouble breathing, coupled with chest pains. Diabetes can immediately be identified by any medical insignia, including bracelets, anklets, tattoos, which can be found using a head to toe check.

TREATMENT

Assist the victim into semi-prone position, aiding the victim administer their medicine.
