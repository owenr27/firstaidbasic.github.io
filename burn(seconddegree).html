Burn (Second Degree)

Moderately Severe Burn

SYMPTOMS

Skin will have blisters, do not pop them. Skin will have a burning sensation, and feel warm to the touch.

TREATMENT

Run cool water over the affected area of skin. Wrap in gauze, remembering to only add, never to remove the gauze. If the burn is bigger than the size of your hand, or on the victim's face or neck, call 911 immediately.
