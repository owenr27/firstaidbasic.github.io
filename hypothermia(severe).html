Hypothermia (Severe)

SYMPTOMS

Victim is unconscious, as the brain shuts down to conserve heat and energy. Victim will no longer be shivering.

TREATMENT

Give victim warm blankets, or other heavy clothes. Give victim warm food and drink. Act quickly, as elongated times of hypothermia induced unconsciousness, will lead to severe brain death.
